LET'S GO FISHING!


There is nothing more relaxing than a memorable morning on a beautiful Pacific Northwest lake. I supply everything you'll need for the morning. Rods, bait and lures, even breakfast! All you need to bring is yourself. But, if you're excited to try out your own gear, you're welcome to bring it along.


Guests will be fishing for:

  • Rainbow Trout
  • Cutthroat Trout
  • Largemouth Bass
  • Perch
  • Pumpkinseed
  • Bluegill
  • And more...
